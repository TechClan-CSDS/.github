### About

**TechClan** is the official technology club for the **CSE (Data Science)** branch at **Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering**.  
We focus on hands-on learning, open-source collaboration, and skill development through projects and workshops.

---

### Domains We Explore

- Web Development  
- Data Science and Analytics  
- Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence  
- Cloud Computing and DevOps  
- Hackathons and Collaborative Projects  

---

### Get Involved

We encourage students to learn, build, and share knowledge.  
All TechClan repositories and resources are open for exploration and learning.
